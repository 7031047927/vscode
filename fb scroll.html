Netaji Subhas

Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose was an Indian nationalist and freedom fighter. He was born on 23 January 1897 in Cuttack, Odisha, and died on 18 August 1945 in a plane crash in Taiwan.

Netaji was one of the most prominent leaders in the Indian independence movement against British rule. He is known for his famous slogan "Give me blood, and I shall give you freedom".
